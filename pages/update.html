3.1: the third point one

Githack and other caching proxys have been blocked by linewize, goguardian, etc. Meaning that 90% of Luma's games and 7% of Luma's images have been blocked. Due to this constant downtime I am deprecating Githack and relying fully on Cloudflare Pages for game hosting. This means for you that all saves except for roms and flash games are gone, hopefully with Cloudflare this issue doesn't happen as often.

Due to the deprecation of Githack, I lose the advantage of having file sizes above 25MB. As a result, many games may be temporarily or permanently broken. Here are the ones that I am aware of: Biters.io, Scrap Metal 3, Traffic Jam 3D, Quake 1 (maybe), Burrito Bison, Drift Hunters, Majora's Mask, Ocarina of Time, Tanuki Sunset, The Binding of Isaac, Subway Surfers, and 10 Minutes Until Dawn.
